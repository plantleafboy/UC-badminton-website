FAQ!

What equipment do I need to bring?
You can simply bring yourself and preferably some indoor court shoes, we have racquets and shuttles provided.
Where is the Gymnasium?
We are located at the University of Canterbury Recreation centre close to campus
How does the membership discount card work?
Simply present your Membership discount card to any of our amazing sponsors and they will apply the discount for you!
Do I need to be a member to play?
No, non-members can play too. It will be good to come earlier to guarantee a spot!
How can I register?
You can register using this form here or contact us directly if you are having troubles
How does it work there?
We have 4 courts and allocate players to each court and play for 12 minutes before we make the next game. Usually, you play one game and then rest one game.
